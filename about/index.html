    Academic History

    I recently graduated from Ohio State in May 2017 with a BS in Atmospheric Science and minor in Environmental Science. During my undergraduate career, I conducted research in the School of Earth Sciences, was employed through the Office of Research, and worked as a student research assistant at the Byrd Polar and Climate Research Center. I was also involved with Meteorology Club and Womens Glee at Ohio State.

    Future Goals
